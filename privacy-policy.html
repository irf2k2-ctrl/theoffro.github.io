OFFRO Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Welcome to OFFRO.

OFFRO ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the OFFRO mobile application and related services.

By using OFFRO, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Mobile phone number
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Email address (if provided)

Location Information

With your permission, OFFRO collects your device location to:

  • Discover nearby stores
  • Show relevant offers
  • Display nearby products
  • Provide navigation to merchant locations
  • Improve search results

Location access can be disabled at any time from your device settings.

Merchant Information

  • Store name
  • Business category
  • Store address
  • GPS coordinates
  • Business images
  • Product details
  • Offer information
  • Banner images
  • Subscription information

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Performance diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify login using OTP
  • Display nearby stores and offers
  • Improve search accuracy
  • Enable navigation to stores
  • Process merchant subscriptions
  • Display merchant products and offers
  • Improve app performance
  • Detect fraud and prevent misuse
  • Respond to customer support requests

3. Location Data

OFFRO uses your location only to improve your experience by finding nearby businesses, showing local deals, calculating distances, and providing navigation assistance. We do not continuously track your location in the background unless you explicitly grant such permission.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only with service providers that help operate OFFRO, payment providers (where applicable), or government authorities when legally required.

5. Data Security

  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • OTP authentication
  • Restricted database access
  • Server-side security controls

Although we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud. You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

OFFRO is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

  • Update your profile
  • Edit merchant information
  • Disable location permission
  • Request account deletion
  • Contact us regarding your data

9. Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps
  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase (Authentication, Notifications, Analytics, Crash Reporting)
  • Payment gateways (where applicable)

10. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics technologies to improve user experience. The mobile application may use anonymous analytics to improve performance and reliability.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes become effective immediately after publication on our website.
